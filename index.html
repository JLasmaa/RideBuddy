About the Project

The inspiration behind RideBuddy comes from the need to reduce the number of cars on the road, which in turn reduces traffic congestion and air pollution. The app is designed to be user-friendly and easy to navigate, making it easy for users to find and connect with other riders. The app also provides a platform for users to share their experiences and feedback, which helps to improve the overall user experience.
